A Bundled Edge View of the Github Event Graph

Events occur in patterns, as indicated here by coloured groups. This view contains Gist events. Gist Events are no longer produced, but their history remains. They are only visible from an Actor perspective because they are not associated with a repository. Nonetheless, a Gist event may be followed by a repository event. Although clearly they are more likely to accompany other Gist events. Here is a Python Repository's point of view.
